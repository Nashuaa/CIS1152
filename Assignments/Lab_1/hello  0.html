Home

This is my website for CIS 1151. The final intended purpose of my site is to have a site that allows a user to get on, sign into an account, and send audio clips to other users, while being able to recieve clips from those other users.

As a user, the website would give you a friends list to add or delete friends with, and where you could chose a friend to send an audio clip to. This clip could be selected from your computer, or created on the spot via the user's microphone.
